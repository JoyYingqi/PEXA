About Me

I am a UX/UI designer transitioning into software development, driven by a deep passion for building seamless digital experiences that merge design and technology. Based in Melbourne, Australia, I bring a unique blend of creativity and analytical thinking, supported by a Master's degree in Marketing and an Associate degree in Graphic Design. My design background has sharpened my ability to craft intuitive and visually engaging interfaces, and my growing expertise in development enables me to turn these designs into functional, high-performing applications. I am eager to bridge the gap between design and development, continuously expanding my technical skills to create impactful, user-centered digital solutions.
