The journey of becoming a developer is challenging,

but with passion, persistence, and an endless curiosity for problem-solving,
I embrace every step.

1- Discovering My Passion for Development

During my UX/UI design internship, I had the opportunity to work closely with developers. Seeing how they tackled real-world problems through code sparked my curiosity about software development. I realised that beyond creating intuitive designs, I wanted to build solutions that make a real impact.

2- Laying the Foundation

To explore this newfound interest, I applied for and was accepted into the Generation Australia coding bootcamp, a competitive, free program that required a selection process, including a test and interview. Through this program, I built a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This immersive learning experience confirmed my passion for coding and problem-solving, motivating me to pursue a deeper understanding of software development.

3- Advancing My Technical Knowledge (In Progress)

To move beyond the basics, my next goal is to intensely study software development and gain a deeper understanding of programming principles, system architecture, and problem-solving techniques. I am exploring structured learning programs, including full-time study opportunities, to build a strong technical foundation for my future career.

4- Applying My Skills in the Real World (Future Goal)

Ultimately, I want to apply my skills in a professional setting, creating meaningful and impactful software solutions. I aim to gain industry experience in a company that values innovation and problem-solving, where I can contribute as a developer while continuing to grow and refine my expertise.
